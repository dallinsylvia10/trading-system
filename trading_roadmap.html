Phase 03 — Risk Management
03
Master Position Sizing
This is what separates profitable traders from everyone else
WEEK 3–5
  • Learn the 1% rule — never risk more than 1% of your account on a single trade
  • Calculate position size before every trade — Entry price, SL distance, account size → position size
  • Practice on paper with a simulated $1,000 account — how many shares/units can you buy?
  • Add a 4H timeframe to your routine — compare signal quality vs 1H
  • Start tracking R-multiples — a 1R win means you made exactly what you risked. Aim for 2R average wins
✅ Unlock Phase 4 when: You can calculate exact position size in under 30 seconds and your average win is above 1.5R.

Phase 05 — Go Live
05
Fund Your Live Account
You've earned this. Now protect it.
MONTH 2–3
  • Start with $200–$500 maximum — only money you can afford to lose completely
  • Trade exactly like your paper account — same rules, same position size, same timeframe
  • Do not increase position size for the first 20 live trades — emotions will be different, give yourself time
  • Set a daily loss limit — if you lose 3% in one day, close the platform and come back tomorrow
  • Withdraw 50% of every profit milestone — hitting +20%? Take half out. Protect your gains.
✅ You've made it when: 3 consecutive profitable months on your live account with consistent risk management. Then and only then, scale up.

Golden Rules — Never Break These
🚫 No Signal = No TradeIf there's no arrow on the chart, you do not enter. Period. Boredom is not a signal.
🚫 Never Move Your SLOnce set, the stop loss stays. Moving it further away turns a small loss into an account killer.
🚫 1% Risk MaxOne trade should never make or break your account. Size your positions correctly every single time.
🚫 No Revenge TradingJust lost a trade? Step away. The worst trades happen right after a loss when emotions are running hot.
✅ Journal Every TradeIf it's not logged it didn't happen. Your journal is your edge — it shows you patterns you can't see in the moment.
✅ Trust the SystemYou built this indicator for a reason. Give it at least 50 trades before making any changes to the settings.
